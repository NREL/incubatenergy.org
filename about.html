About Us

Through a partnership between the Electric Power Research Institute and the U.S. Department of Energy's National Renewable Energy Laboratory, the Clean Energy Incubator Network provides nationwide coordination among clean energy business incubators.

The network includes members from all over the country who attended our February 2015 kickoff workshop in Washington D.C., including the Austin Technology Incubator (ATI), Clean Energy Trust (CET) in Chicago, CLT Joules in Charlotte, the Energy Excelerator from Hawaii, Greentown Labs from Boston, the Los Angeles Cleantech Incubator (LACI) in California, NextEnergy in Detroit, NYC Acre in the northeast, Oregon BEST in the northwest, and Prospect Silicon Valley in the Bay Area.

The New England Clean Energy Council (NECEC) helps coordinate, along with MassCEC in Massachusetts, clean energy resources in the Northeast, and the Cleantech Open has regional chapters all over the country.

For more information about the Clean Energy Incubator Network, contact us.
